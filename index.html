About Me

I am an aspiring interior designer and graphic designer with a passion for creating visually compelling and functional spaces. With a background in Interior Architecture, I have developed a strong understanding of design principles, space planning, and the integration of architecture and interior design.

Currently pursuing a degree in Architectural Design, I am expanding my knowledge and skills in design theory, sustainability, and innovative building techniques.

My experience in graphic design complements my interior design studies, allowing me to approach projects from a unique perspective. I enjoy blending aesthetics and functionality, ensuring that every design element works harmoniously together.

I am particularly interested in sustainable design practices and the incorporation of natural elements into modern architecture. Through internships and hands-on projects, I have gained valuable experience in collaborating with clients and teams, honing my ability to communicate ideas clearly and effectively.

Eager to contribute my creativity and enthusiasm to a dynamic design team, I aim to continue learning and growing in the field. My goal is to create spaces that not only meet the needs of their occupants but also inspire and uplift.
